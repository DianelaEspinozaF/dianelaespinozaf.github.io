This book summarises new findings and pending challenges from the Young Lives longitudinal study in Peru, covering the period 2018-2025. I led the writing of the education section, drawing on data from over 2,000 participants followed since early childhood.

**Authors:** Cueto, S., Rojas, V., Espinoza, D., Campos, A., & Curi, K.

**Publisher:** GRADE, Group for the Analysis of Development, Lima.
