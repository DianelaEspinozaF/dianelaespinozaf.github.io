This paper evaluates whether Peru's conditional cash transfer programme (JUNTOS) impacts long-term human capital and family formation outcomes using propensity score matching on longitudinal data (2006-2023) from 2,000+ Young Lives participants.

Key outcomes evaluated include secondary school completion, higher education transition, labour market entry, and early parenthood at age 22.

**Authors:**  Woodman, A., Espinoza, D. & Sánchez, A.
