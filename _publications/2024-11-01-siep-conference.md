Presented preliminary results of the Growing JUNTOS working paper at the IX National Seminar organized by the Peruvian Society of Educational Research (SIEP) in Tarapoto, Peru.

The presentation covered the research design, propensity score matching methodology, and early findings on the long-term impacts of Peru's JUNTOS conditional cash transfer programme on human capital outcomes including secondary school completion, higher education transition, and labour market entry.
